What we work on

Research foci

Six method families, each an instance of the same framework. Read across the equations and the family resemblance is the point.

Multilevel SEM

Nested latent structure

Separating within- and between-cluster covariance so classroom, learner, and item effects are not confounded.

\( \boldsymbol{\Sigma}_T=\boldsymbol{\Sigma}_W+\boldsymbol{\Sigma}_B \)
HLM

Random-coefficient models

Slopes and intercepts that vary across higher-level units, capturing context as a source of systematic variation.

\( \mathbf{y}=\mathbf{X}\boldsymbol{\beta}+\mathbf{Z}\mathbf{u}+\boldsymbol{\varepsilon} \)
Adaptive testing · CAT

Information-maximizing measurement

Each item is chosen to maximize information at the provisional ability estimate, shortening tests without losing precision.

\( j^{*}=\arg\max_j\, I_j(\hat{\theta}) \)
Meta-analysis

Synthesis across studies

Random-effects and Bayesian meta-synthesis that model heterogeneity rather than averaging it away.

\( \hat{\delta}_i=\delta+u_i+e_i,\ u_i\sim\mathcal{N}(0,\tau^2) \)
Data & text mining

Learning representations

Dimension reduction, topic models, and neural encoders that turn corpora and behavioural logs into interpretable coordinates.

\( \boldsymbol{\theta}\leftarrow\boldsymbol{\theta}-\eta\,\nabla_{\boldsymbol{\theta}}\mathcal{L} \)
Dynamical modelling

Affect over time

Differential-equation models of how motivation, engagement, and anxiety drive one another along a learning trajectory. Coupled ODEs let us trace regime shifts that snapshots miss, mapping how learner states settle into attractors and escape from local minima.

\( \dot{\mathbf{x}}=\mathbf{f}(\mathbf{x};\boldsymbol{\theta}),\quad \mathbf{x}(0)=\mathbf{x}_0 \)
